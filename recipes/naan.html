Na'an

These are great for making flatbreads, using for gyros or pitas. They can be cut in half and made into a pock ot cut into pieces and fried for pita chips. My favorite is reheating them on the grill and tearing them apart to dip in hummus.

For this recipe you will need:

  • Kitchen scale
  • Stand mixer with dough hook
  • Large bowl
  • Dough scraper
  • Rolling pin
  • Cooling rack

This recipe makes 6 x 8" flatbreads.

Ingredients

% g Ingredient
100 811 00 Flour or Bread Flour
60 486 Water
2 16 Salt
1 8 Instant Yeast
2 16 Sugar
5 41 Olive Oil
15 122 Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Add water, yeast, sugar, oil, and yogurt to the stand mixer and mix on low until combine.
  2. Add the flour and continue to mix on low until the dough comes together.
  3. Continue mixing on low for 3 minutes, then set speed to medium and mix for 8 - 10 minutes.
  4. Place dough into a large lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and allow it to bulk ferment for 2 hours or until doubled.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work space and with your bench scraper portion the dough into 250g pieces.
  6. Pulling the corners of each portioned piece into the center, turning it smooth side up and rolling it gently into a ball, shape each portion.
  7. Lightly flour and cover your portioned dough balls and allow them to rest for 30 - 45 minutes.
  8. There are three ways to cook these flatbreads
    • On a clean, lightly oiled grill
    • In a cast iron or heavy bottom stainless steal pan on the stove. Lightly oiled and set to medium - medium-low head
    • In the oven at 475°F on a stone or similar heavy oven safe pan.
  9. Setup your prefered cooking method by preheating your oven, pan , or grill.
  10. Roll out your portioned dough balls to roughly 8" and 1 cm thick.
  11. Place the flatbread into your pan or onto your grill.
  12. Once it begins to puff flip the flatbread and cook on the other side. If it blows up like a baloon that means it is done.
  13. Remove the flatbread and lay it on a cooling rack and continue cooking the rest of the flatbreads.
  14. Serve warm or allow to cool completely and store air tight for later.
