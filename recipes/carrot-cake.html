Carrot Cake

This carrot cake is one of the best I've ever had. Super moist and delicious. You'd never know there were vegetables in it.

For this recipe you'll need:

  • Box Grater
  • Kitchen Scale
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Stand mixer with whisk and paddle attachments
  • 8" round pan

This Recipe makes one 8" cake and enough icing to turn it into a two layer cake.

Recipe

Qty. Ingredient
75g Walnuts or Pecans, Fine Chopped
75g Walnuts or Pecans, Rough Chopped
454g Carrots, Fine Grated
240g Buttermilk
1 Tbsp Ginger, Fresh Grated
2 tsp Vanilla Extract
325g All Purpose Flour
1 Tbsp Cinnamone
8g Baking Powder
6g Salt
6g Baking Soda
1 tsp Ginger, Ground
1/4 tsp Clove, Ground
200g Eggs
150g Sugar
150g Brown Sugar
226g Nuetral Oil

Instructions:

  1. Set oven to 350°F
  2. Oil the cake pan and cut a piece of parchemnt to fit the bottom of the pan. Place it in the pan and apply oil to the parchemnt aslo.
  3. Combine the carrots, buttermilk, fresh ginger, and vanilla in a bowl.
  4. Combine the finely crushed nuts, flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, salt, ground ginger, and clove in a separate bowl.
  5. Beat the eggs and both sugars on high speed until they reach ribbon stage in your stand mixer with the whisk attachment.
  6. Switching the mixer to medium, stream in the oil until incorporated.
  7. Switch to the paddle attachment and on a low speed alternate adding the dry and wet ingredients.
  8. Finally fold in the rough chopped nuts and pour into the prepared pan.
  9. Place the pan on the middle rack of the oven and bake 25-28 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean from the center.
  10. allow cake to cool in the pan.
  11. While cooling prepare the Cream Cheese Icing
  12. Once the cake is completely cool cut in half evenly to get two identical rounds.
  13. Pipe icing in a zigzag fashion fully covering the top of the first round and place the second round upside down ontop of the first.
  14. Apply Icing to the top of the cake in a similar zigzag fashion.
  15. Finish cake by sprinkling with crushed nuts and a light dusting of cinnamon.
