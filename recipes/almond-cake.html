Almond Cake

This is a true crowd pleaser. It pairs perfectly with seasonal macerated fruit and whipped creme fraiche.

For this recipe you will need:

  • 9" or 10" Springform pan
  • Stand mixer with whisk attachment
  • Kitchen scale

Ingredients

280g Butter
128g All Purpose Flour
125g Almond Flour
125g Powdered Sugar
6 Lg Eggs
1 Lg Egg Whites
1/2 tsp Almond Extract
1/2 tsp Salt
250g Sugar
2 tsp Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 Tbsp Baking Powder

Instructions

  1. Set your oven to 325°F.
  2. Butter and flour your springform pan and apply parchment to the bottom of the pan.
  3. Mix your almond flour and powder sugar on low in the stand mixer.
  4. Add salt and egg whites and mix on high to form a paste, scrap down the bowl often from this point on.
  5. Mix in the sugar on medium speed until the mixture becomes crumbly.
  6. Add the room temperature butter and vanilla then mix on high until light and fluffy.
  7. With the mixer running on medium add the eggs one at a time stopping half way through to scrape down the bowl.
  8. Last add the flour and baking powder on low speed until incorporated, scraping the bowl once more.
  9. Pour into spring form pan and smooth ou the top.
  10. Bake for 60 - 70 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean from the center.
  11. Allow to cool completely before removing from the pan and serving.
