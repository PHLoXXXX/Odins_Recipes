Cream Cheese Icing

This icing is a mix between buttercream and yoru classic cream cheese icing. It still has that tangy cream cheese flavor but is less sweet and more rich than the classic icing.

This recipe will require:

  • Kitchen Scale
  • Stand Mixer with Paddle Attachment
  • Measuring Spoons

Make sure you're butter and cream cheese are at room temperature for this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 227g Butter, room temp
  • 454g Cream Cheese, room temp
  • 454g Powdered Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • To Taste Salt

Instructions:

  1. Beat the butter and cream cheese on high speed in the stand mixer using the paddle attachment until smooth and lightened slightly.
  2. On low speed add the powdered sugar.
  3. Once incorporated beat on high until light and fluffy.
  4. On medium add the vanilla.
